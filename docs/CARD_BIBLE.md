# Arcane Backlash Card Bible

## Review Policy

Each pack grows in blocks of ten cards. At 30 cards, it receives a full mechanical review. Similar ideas may remain during early development and are corrected during that review.

## Early Review Watchlist

The following relationships should be examined when the pack reaches 30 cards:

- MR-003 Feedback Pulse and MR-008 Crossed Intent both influence a later spell attack.
- MR-006 Broken Cadence and MR-010 Empty Follow-Through both alter action sequencing.
- MR-001 Arcane Recoil and MR-005 Energy Grounding both influence movement.
- MR-002 Resonant Hands and MR-009 Forced Recalculation both create conditional follow-up costs.

These cards currently create different tactical choices, but the full review will determine whether later additions make any of them redundant.
