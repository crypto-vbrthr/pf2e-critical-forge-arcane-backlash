# Arcane Backlash Card Bible

## Review Policy

Each pack grows in blocks of ten cards. At 30 cards, it receives a full mechanical review. Similar ideas may remain during early development and are corrected during that review.

The complete draft remains predominantly moderate, with two short-lived strong results and three deliberately dry humorous cards. All 30 cards are manual because their consequences depend on immediate choices, target-specific triggers, geometry, action sequencing, or conditional consumption.

## Early Review Watchlist

The following relationships should be examined when the pack reaches 30 cards:

- MR-003 Feedback Pulse and MR-008 Crossed Intent both influence a later spell attack.
- MR-006 Broken Cadence and MR-010 Empty Follow-Through both alter action sequencing.
- MR-001 Arcane Recoil and MR-005 Energy Grounding both influence movement.
- MR-002 Resonant Hands and MR-009 Forced Recalculation both create conditional follow-up costs.
- MR-003 Feedback Pulse and MR-020 Magical Tell both improve the target's defense against a later spell.
- MR-008 Crossed Intent, MR-012 Unstable Casting Ground, and MR-013 Collapsed Angle all reward relocation.
- MR-007 Lingering Syllable, MR-017 Luminous Outline, and MR-018 Echoing Presence all reveal or simplify locating the caster.
- MR-015 Folded Distance and later range-manipulation cards should be compared for practical usefulness.
- MR-016 Afterimage and MR-019 Colors Out of Order both use concealment, but in opposite directions.
- MR-021 Frayed Pattern should be compared with MR-001 Arcane Recoil and MR-005 Energy Grounding for movement overlap.
- MR-024 Spell Snag, MR-026 Shadow Arrives Late, and MR-030 Reality Takes Notes all grant the original target a reactive answer to later casting.
- MR-025 Unfinished Ending should be compared with MR-006 Broken Cadence for action-sequence overlap.
- MR-027 Voice of the Wrong Element and MR-029 Applause from Nowhere join the audible-signature cluster around MR-007 and MR-018.
- MR-028 Familiar Geometry is intentionally symmetrical, but its table speed and practical impact require review.

These cards currently create different tactical choices, but the full review will determine whether later additions make any of them redundant.
