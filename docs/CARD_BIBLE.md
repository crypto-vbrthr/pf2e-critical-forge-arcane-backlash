# Arcane Backlash Card Bible

## Review Policy

Each pack grows in blocks of ten cards. At 30 cards, it receives a full mechanical review. Similar ideas may remain during early development and are corrected during that review.

## Miscast Repercussions - Mechanical Review

**Status:** Passed after revision in version 0.1.3

The review replaced seven redundant or unreliable mechanics and clarified five timing, visibility, range, and geometry cases. Card IDs remain stable, including MR-029, whose displayed title changed from *Applause from Nowhere* to *Unsolicited Footnotes*.

## Review Outcome

The revised pack no longer duplicates the later-spell-defense, relocate-for-a-modifier, audible-location, consecutive-concentrate, applause, or reactive-Step mechanics identified in the 0.1.2 review. The pack retains a deliberate mixture of magical recoil, battlefield residue, false origins, predictive defenses, warped perception, and dry magical humor.
